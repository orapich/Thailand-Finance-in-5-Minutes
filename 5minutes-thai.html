About

Hi — I'm Ernearn, a Thai student studying in London, seven hours behind the news back home. I started 5 Minutes Thai Updates because I kept drifting away from what was happening in Thailand's economy while buried in coursework and global finance content.

This newsletter is my way of staying connected. Each week, I handpick the most significant stories affecting Thailand's economy and finance, do the research, and distill it into a fast, readable update — with real numbers, clear context, and a concrete takeaway.

The goal isn't deep economic analysis. It's to make sure that anyone who cares about Thailand's financial future stays informed, even if they only have five minutes.
